Admissions

Ozone High School welcomes students who are committed to academic excellence, discipline, and personal growth. The admissions process is designed to ensure that every student is prepared to succeed in a supportive and structured learning environment.

Requirements

Prepare these documents before submitting your admission application.

  • Completed application form
  • Previous school transcripts or report cards
  • Copy of birth certificate or national ID
  • One passport-size photograph
  • Parent/guardian contact information
