Our Staff

Ozone High School is proud to have a team of highly qualified and dedicated professionals committed to academic excellence and student success.
Our staff members work collaboratively to create a positive, disciplined, and supportive learning environment.

School Leadership

  • Principal: Provides overall leadership and strategic direction.
  • Vice Principal: Supports academic and administrative operations.
  • Academic Coordinator: Oversees curriculum and academic performance.

Teaching Staff

Our experienced teachers specialize in various subjects including Mathematics, Natural Sciences, Social Sciences, Languages, and Information Technology.
They are committed to delivering quality education and mentoring students for future success.

Administrative Staff

The administrative team ensures efficient management of student records, admissions, attendance, and communication through the school’s Record Management System.

Together, our staff members uphold the values of discipline, integrity, professionalism, and continuous improvement.
